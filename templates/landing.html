NYC Building Energy Explorer

What's in the Data?
  • Energy Intensity: Energy use from all sources per sq. ft of building area. Lower is better. Measured in kBtu/ft2.
  • Energy Intensity Benchmark: Median energy intensity for a particular property type (e.g. Hospital) nationally.
  • Water Use Intensity: Water use per sq. ft. Measured in gal/ft2.
  • Greenhouse Gas Emissions: Total greenhouse gas emissions measured in metric tons of CO2.2.
Greater, Greener Buildings Plan

In New York City, 68% of greenhouse gas emissions come from buildings. Nearly half of those emissions come from just 2% of buildings. The Greater, Greener Buildings Plan addresses this problem. Local Law 84 also requires that large building report data on energy and water use. The law applies to municipal buildings over 10,000 sq. ft. and all buildings over 25,000 sq. ft. This app explores data for 2019.

App Purpose: This app compares each building's energy intensity to the national benchmark for its property type. Choose a property type to begin exploring.
